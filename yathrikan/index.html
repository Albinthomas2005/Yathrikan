Features

Smarter Commutes Start Here

Cutting-edge technology meets daily transit to save you time and stress.

🛰️
Live AI Tracking

Our proprietary neural network predicts bus arrival times with 99.3% accuracy using a multi-layered shadow model that learns traffic patterns in real-time.

💳
Contactless Tickets

Scan and go — our NFC technology lets you board without ever reaching for your physical wallet. Powered by Razorpay for secure, instant payments.

🗺️
Route Optimization

Dynamic pathfinding finds the fastest route based on live conditions — road closures, weather, and sudden transit delays recalculated instantly.

🎙️
AI Voice Assistant

Speak naturally in English or Malayalam. Our bilingual voice assistant handles route queries, ticket booking, and navigation hands-free.

🔔
Smart Notifications

Never miss your bus again. Get personalized arrival alerts, route change warnings, and service disruption notices pushed directly to your device.

How It Works

Three Steps to Smarter Travel

From download to departure in under a minute.

1
Download & Sign In

Install Yathrikan and sign in with Google in just one tap. Your preferences, saved routes, and ticket history sync instantly across devices.

2
Search Your Route

Type, tap, or speak your destination. Our AI shows all nearby buses, live on the map, with precise ETAs and seat availability in seconds.

3
Board & Go

Use your digital QR ticket to board, track your journey in real-time, and get off at exactly the right stop with guided turn-by-turn alerts.

Technology

Built on a Solid Foundation

Flutter & Dart
